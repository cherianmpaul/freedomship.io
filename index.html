The Concept

Simple. Beautiful. Free.

FreedomShip reimagines how human beings can live — on the ocean, in community, with every need met and the entire world to explore. Here is exactly how it works.

01

Join the Waiting List

Sign up today — it's completely free and non-binding. Cabins are allocated strictly on a first-come, first-served basis. The earlier you join, the sooner you could be living free on the ocean. Tell us your family size and cabin needs when you sign up.

02

Receive Your Cabin

When a cabin becomes available and it's your turn on the list, we contact you to confirm your allocation. Families needing multiple cabins are always accommodated together. Once your cabin is assigned, it is yours for as long as you choose to stay.

Yours for life
03

Everything Is Free

From the moment you step onboard, everything is free — your cabin, all meals at every restaurant, every recreational facility, all entertainment, all shops. The ship travels the world following perfect weather. You never pay for anything, ever.

100% free, always
04

Love Is the Only Currency

There is no money onboard. The only currency is the love and kindness you show your neighbours. Zero tolerance for bad behaviour — anyone who harms the community is permanently banned from all FreedomShip vessels, with no exceptions.

Kindness first
05

Stay as Long as You Wish

No minimum stays. No contracts. No obligations. Your cabin is yours indefinitely. If you choose to leave, your cabin is returned to the community for the next person on the list. Your freedom to stay — or go — is absolute.

No strings attached

Questions

Everything you need to know.

Is FreedomShip really completely free?
+
Yes — completely and unconditionally free. Your cabin, all meals, all facilities, all entertainment, all shops. You pay nothing, ever. The ships are funded through FreedomShip Coin (FSC) purchased by supporters around the world who believe in this vision.
How does the waiting list work?
+
You join today, for free. Cabins are assigned in strict first-come, first-served order — the earlier you join, the closer to the front of the queue you are. When a cabin is available and it's your turn, we contact you. Families needing multiple adjacent cabins are always accommodated together.
Can I leave FreedomShip whenever I want?
+
Absolutely. There are no minimum stays, no contracts, and no penalties. Your cabin is yours for as long as you choose to stay. If you leave, the cabin returns to the community and is offered to the next person on the waiting list. You may rejoin the waiting list at any time, though you won't retain your previous position.
What does "love is the currency" mean in practice?
+
There is no money onboard FreedomShip. Everything is provided freely. In return, the only expectation is that you treat every fellow community member with kindness, respect, and love. Any behaviour that harms, threatens, or disrespects another community member results in permanent removal from all FreedomShip vessels, with no exceptions and no appeal.
Does buying FreedomShip Coin give me priority on the list?
+
No — not at all. The FreedomShip Coin (FSC) is a way to financially support the project, similar to a donation. It gives no priority or advantage in cabin allocation whatsoever. The waiting list is purely first-come, first-served based on registration date. This ensures fairness for everyone, regardless of financial means.
What is the FreedomShip Coin (FSC) and Holochain?
+
FSC is a limited-supply digital currency running on the Holochain network. Holochain is an agent-centric distributed system where each participant maintains their own cryptographically signed chain of actions — validated peer-to-peer rather than through a single global ledger. This means FSC transactions have no gas fees and the network is genuinely community-owned rather than controlled by any central party.
Who decides where the ship goes?
+
The crew plans the route based on seasonal weather patterns and continuous guest feedback. The goal is always to be at the location with the most beautiful climate at that time of year. Guests can submit preferences which feed directly into routing decisions. The route is a living document — never fixed, always optimised for the community's experience.
Can families and children join?
+
Absolutely — FreedomShip is designed for all ages and all family sizes. Multiple cabins can be allocated for families, and our ships include dedicated schools, childcare facilities, playgrounds, youth programmes, and family spaces. Simply indicate your family size and cabin requirements when you join the waiting list and your group will be accommodated together.
Can I volunteer onboard if I can't buy FSC coins?
+
Yes — volunteering is a full and equal way to support the FreedomShip mission. Volunteers contribute their skills in hospitality, education, maintenance, entertainment, childcare, and many other areas. Volunteering carries no special privileges — it is simply an expression of care for the community. As automation increases, the nature of volunteering will evolve, but your contribution is always honoured and celebrated.
